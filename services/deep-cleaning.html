Deep Cleaning Services

A comprehensive, top-to-bottom reset for your property. We tackle built-up limescale, grease, and grime that accumulates over time. Kitchens, bathrooms, and high-touch areas receive intensive attention—surfaces are sanitised, fixtures polished, and hard-to-reach spots cleaned thoroughly. Ideal before special events, seasonal refreshes, or when you need a complete property reset.

What's Included

  • Kitchen degreasing: Hobs, extractors, splashbacks, and worktops scrubbed to remove grease buildup
  • Bathroom descaling: Taps, tiles, grout, shower heads, and screens cleaned of limescale and soap residue
  • Internal window cleaning: Glass, frames, and sills cleaned inside (external available as add-on)
  • Skirting and architrave: Woodwork wiped, dust removed from edges and corners
  • Door and cupboard cleaning: Handles, panels, and interiors (if requested) wiped down
  • Light fixtures and switches: Dust removed, switches sanitised
  • Behind furniture: Accessible areas vacuumed and wiped (movable furniture only)
  • All standard cleaning tasks: Plus vacuuming, mopping, dusting throughout

How It Works

1

Book & Brief

Contact us with your property size and any specific concerns (stubborn stains, particular areas needing attention). We'll provide an estimate and arrange a convenient date. If needed, we'll visit to assess the work required.

2

Deep Clean

Our team arrives with specialist equipment and products. We systematically work through each room, focusing on areas that need intensive attention. We scrub, descale, degrease, sanitise, and polish until everything meets our high standards.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does deep cleaning take?

It depends on property size and condition. A typical 2-bedroom flat takes 4-6 hours, while larger properties may require a full day or two visits. We'll provide an accurate estimate after assessing your requirements.

What's the difference between deep cleaning and regular cleaning?

Regular cleaning maintains surface-level cleanliness. Deep cleaning targets built-up dirt, limescale, grease, and grime in hard-to-reach areas. It includes intensive scrubbing, descaling, degreasing, and sanitisation.

How often should I book deep cleaning?

Most clients schedule deep cleaning 2-4 times per year, or before special occasions, seasonal changes, or when moving house. It complements regular cleaning to maintain thorough hygiene.

Do I need to be home during deep cleaning?

No, though you're welcome to be present. Many clients provide access and leave us to work. We'll update you on progress and notify you when complete.
