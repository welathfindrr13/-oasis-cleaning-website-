Residential Cleaning Services

Reliable, professional home cleaning tailored to your schedule and preferences. From kitchens and bathrooms to living areas and bedrooms, we leave every room fresh, sanitised, and dust-free. Whether you need a weekly routine or a one-off deep clean, our trained team delivers consistent results you can trust.

What's Included

  • Kitchen cleaning: Worktops, hobs, sinks, splashbacks, and appliance exteriors wiped and sanitised
  • Bathroom sanitisation: Toilets, basins, taps, tiles, mirrors, and shower/bath cleaned and descaled
  • Living areas: Dusting of surfaces, shelves, and furniture; vacuuming carpets and rugs; mopping hard floors
  • Bedrooms: Bed making (if requested), surface dusting, vacuuming or mopping floors
  • Hallways and stairs: Vacuuming, mopping, handrail wiping
  • Waste removal: Emptying bins and replacing liners throughout the property
  • Skirting and architrave: Dusting and wiping down visible woodwork
  • Eco-friendly products: Safe, effective cleaning solutions used throughout

How It Works

1

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I book residential cleaning?

It depends on your household size, lifestyle, and preferences. Most clients choose weekly or fortnightly cleans for consistent maintenance. Monthly cleans work well for smaller flats or less busy households.

What rooms do you clean?

We clean all rooms you specify—kitchens, bathrooms, bedrooms, living rooms, hallways, and stairs. You can customise which areas receive attention each visit.

Do I need to be home during the clean?

No. Many clients provide access via key safe, lockbox, or arrange key collection. We follow strict security protocols and all staff are fully vetted.

What if I'm not satisfied with the clean?

We offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If anything isn't right, contact us within 24 hours and we'll return to re-clean the area at no extra cost.
