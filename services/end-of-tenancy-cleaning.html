End of Tenancy Cleaning Services

Comprehensive, inventory-friendly cleaning designed to meet check-out standards and maximise your chances of securing your full deposit. We clean appliances, skirtings, internal windows, and fixtures to the thorough standard expected by landlords and letting agents. Whether you're a tenant moving out or a landlord preparing for new tenants, we ensure the property is spotless.

What's Included

  • Full property deep clean: All rooms cleaned to high standard including kitchens, bathrooms, bedrooms, living areas
  • Kitchen appliances: Oven exterior, hob, extractor, fridge, freezer, dishwasher (exteriors) cleaned and polished
  • Bathroom sanitisation: Toilets, basins, taps, tiles, grout descaled and sanitised; mould treatment where needed
  • Internal windows: Glass, frames, and sills cleaned throughout the property
  • Skirting boards and architraves: Wiped clean, dust and marks removed
  • Doors and door frames: Handles, panels, and frames cleaned
  • Floors: Carpets vacuumed thoroughly, hard floors mopped and polished
  • Cupboards and wardrobes: Interiors wiped, exteriors cleaned

How It Works

1

Book in Advance

Contact us as soon as you have your move-out date. We'll arrange the clean for after you've removed all belongings and furniture. Provide details about property size, any particular concerns, and your check-out date.

2

Comprehensive Clean

Our experienced team arrives with specialist equipment. We systematically work through every room, paying particular attention to areas commonly inspected during check-outs: kitchens, bathrooms, windows, and high-touch surfaces.

3

Ready for Inspection

The property will be clean, fresh, and ready for final inspection. We understand inventory requirements and clean to meet those standards. If any issues arise during inspection, contact us within 48 hours—we guarantee our work.

  • • External window cleaning

Frequently Asked Questions

Will this help me get my deposit back?

Our end of tenancy cleaning meets the standards expected by most landlords and letting agents. We focus on the areas typically checked during inventory inspections. While we can't guarantee deposit return (as this depends on other factors), our thorough clean maximises your chances.

How long does end of tenancy cleaning take?

A 1-bedroom flat typically takes 3-4 hours, a 2-bedroom 4-6 hours, and larger properties 6-8 hours or more. We'll provide an accurate estimate based on your property size and condition.

Do you clean carpets and ovens?

Basic carpet vacuuming and oven exterior cleaning are included. Deep carpet shampooing and intensive oven interior cleaning are available as optional add-ons at additional cost.

When should I book end of tenancy cleaning?

Book as soon as you have your move-out date confirmed. We recommend scheduling for the day after you've moved out all furniture and belongings, and before your final inspection.
