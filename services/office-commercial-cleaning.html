Office & Commercial Cleaning Services

Professional, discreet cleaning for offices, clinics, showrooms, and commercial premises across London. We work around your schedule—after hours or during the day—maintaining consistent standards while respecting your space, clients, and security protocols. From reception desks to meeting rooms, we keep your workspace spotless.

What's Included

  • Desk and workstation cleaning: Surfaces wiped, keyboards dusted, screens cleaned, cables tidied
  • Reception and waiting areas: High-touch surfaces sanitised, floors vacuumed and mopped, magazines tidied
  • Meeting rooms: Tables, chairs, whiteboards cleaned; AV equipment dusted; floor care
  • Breakrooms and kitchens: Worktops, sinks, appliance exteriors, fridges (exterior), tables, and chairs cleaned
  • Washrooms: Toilets, sinks, taps, mirrors, tiles sanitised; consumables restocked (if provided); floors mopped
  • Waste management: Bins emptied, liners replaced, recycling sorted (as per your system)
  • Floors: Carpets vacuumed, hard floors swept and mopped, entrance mats cleaned
  • Security protocols: Alarm setting/disabling, door locking, access logging as required

How It Works

1

Site Assessment & Scheduling

We visit your premises to understand your layout, specific requirements, and security protocols. Together, we agree on a cleaning schedule (daily, weekly, or custom) and set up secure access arrangements.

2

Discreet Cleaning

Our trained team arrives at the agreed time with all equipment. We work efficiently and quietly, respecting your business operations. If working during business hours, we minimise disruption; if after hours, we secure the premises upon leaving.

3

Quality Checks & Reporting

Supervisors conduct regular spot-checks to maintain standards. We provide cleaning logs or reports if required, and you can always reach us if you need adjustments or have concerns.

Optional Add-ons

  • • Window cleaning (internal and external)
  • • Carpet deep cleaning and stain removal
  • • Hard floor stripping, sealing, and polishing
  • • High-level dusting (light fittings, air vents)
  • • Specialist COVID-19 or infection control protocols

Add-ons incur additional charges. Discuss with us when booking.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can you clean during business hours?

Yes, we offer both daytime and after-hours cleaning. Many clients prefer after-hours to avoid disruption, but we can work around your schedule.

How do you handle security and access?

All staff are fully vetted and trained in secure access protocols. We can work with key-holding, alarm codes, or building management systems. Confidentiality and security are paramount.

Do you provide cleaning supplies?

Yes, we bring all necessary equipment and eco-friendly products. If you prefer specific products or have particular requirements, we can accommodate that.

What if we need additional cleaning between scheduled visits?

We offer flexible ad-hoc services for urgent requirements or special events. Contact us and we'll arrange additional cleaning at short notice where possible.
