Specialist Cleaning Services

Targeted cleaning protocols for environments requiring specialist attention. From medical and clinical spaces to post-construction dust removal, we deploy appropriate equipment, products, and procedures. Our team receives additional training for sensitive environments, ensuring we meet specific hygiene standards while respecting clinical guidance, manufacturer instructions, and health and safety requirements.

What's Included

  • Medical and clinical space cleaning: Treatment rooms, waiting areas, and consultation spaces sanitised following infection control guidelines
  • Post-construction cleanup: Fine dust removal with HEPA vacuums, surface wiping, window cleaning, floor preparation
  • High-frequency touchpoint disinfection: Enhanced sanitisation of door handles, light switches, handrails, keypads
  • HEPA filtration cleaning: Removes allergens, fine particles, and contaminants for sensitive environments
  • Specialist surface protocols: Appropriate agents for stainless steel, medical-grade materials, delicate equipment
  • Waste management: Safe handling and disposal of specialist waste in accordance with regulations
  • Documentation: Cleaning logs and records if required for compliance purposes
  • Flexible scheduling: Work around your operational requirements and patient/client schedules

How It Works

1

Consultation & Assessment

We meet to understand your specific requirements, whether medical protocols, post-construction challenges, or other specialist needs. We'll discuss appropriate cleaning agents, timing, access arrangements, and any regulatory requirements you must meet.

2

Protocol Implementation

Our trained team follows the agreed protocol with appropriate equipment and products. For medical spaces, we follow infection control guidance. For post-construction, we use HEPA vacuums and systematic dust removal. We adapt our approach to your environment's specific needs.

3

Quality Assurance & Documentation

We verify the space meets required standards and provide documentation if needed for compliance or audit purposes. Regular reviews ensure protocols remain effective as your needs evolve.

Frequently Asked Questions

What makes specialist cleaning different?

Specialist cleaning uses targeted protocols, specialist equipment (like HEPA filters), and specific cleaning agents designed for sensitive environments. Our staff receive additional training for medical spaces, post-construction sites, and other specialist requirements.

Do you clean medical and clinical spaces?

Yes, we clean medical practices, dental clinics, physiotherapy rooms, and similar healthcare spaces. We follow infection control guidelines and use appropriate sanitisation protocols. For clinical-grade sterilisation, you should use specialist medical cleaning contractors.

Can you handle post-construction dust?

Yes, post-construction cleaning is one of our specialist services. We use HEPA vacuums to remove fine dust particles, clean all surfaces thoroughly, and address the specific challenges of post-renovation cleanup.

Do you provide COVID-19 or infection control cleaning?

Yes, we offer enhanced sanitisation protocols following current health guidance. This includes high-frequency touchpoint disinfection, use of approved sanitising agents, and systematic surface cleaning.
