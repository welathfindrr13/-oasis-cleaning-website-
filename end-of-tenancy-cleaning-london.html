Professional End of Tenancy Cleaning in London

Our end of tenancy cleaning service is designed for tenants, landlords, estate agents and property managers who require a property to be returned in inspection-ready condition.

What's Included

Our end of tenancy cleans are performed using a structured, property-handover checklist including:

  • Full kitchen degreasing (inside ovens, hobs, cupboards and appliances)
  • Bathroom descaling and sanitation
  • Full floor vacuuming and mopping
  • Skirting boards, doors, frames and edges
  • Internal windows, mirrors and glass
  • High-touch point disinfection
  • Waste removal and bin sanitisation

We use professional-grade products designed to remove limescale, grease and built-up residue.
