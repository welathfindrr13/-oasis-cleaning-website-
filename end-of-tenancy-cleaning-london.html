End of Tenancy Cleaning

Move-out cleaning to meet landlord and agent standards

For tenants, landlords, and letting agents who need a clean property ready for final inspection or new occupants.

Request a Quote 020 3750 8878

Who This Service Is For

  • Tenants moving out who want to protect their deposit
  • Landlords preparing properties between tenancies
  • Letting agents needing properties ready for inspection
  • Anyone who needs a clear scope, fixed price and documented standards

What's Included

Kitchen

  • Oven cleaned inside and out (including door glass)
  • Hob and extractor hood degreased
  • Fridge and freezer defrosted and cleaned
  • All cupboards wiped inside and out
  • Worktops, backsplash and sink deep cleaned
  • Appliance exteriors wiped down

Bathrooms

  • Toilet, sink and bath/shower deep cleaned
  • Limescale and soap scum removed
  • Tiles and grout cleaned
  • Mirrors, cabinets and fixtures cleaned
  • Extractor fans wiped

Bedrooms & Living Areas

  • All surfaces dusted and wiped
  • Light fittings and switches cleaned
  • Wardrobes and cupboards cleaned inside
  • Skirting boards wiped throughout
  • Doors, frames and handles cleaned
  • Windowsills cleaned (internal)

Floors Throughout

  • All floors vacuumed and mopped
  • Carpets vacuumed (steam cleaning available separately)
  • Hard floors cleaned to edges

What's Not Included

  • External window cleaning
  • Carpet steam cleaning (available as an add-on)
  • Heavy mould or biological hazard removal
  • Removal of tenant belongings or rubbish
  • Repairs, painting or maintenance work

Pricing

From £300.00 fixed price

Final price depends on property size and condition. All pricing is confirmed in writing before work begins.

Site survey required for accurate quotation.

Optional Add-ons

  • Oven Deep Clean From £70
  • Fridge/Freezer Deep Clean Quote on request
  • Carpet Shampooing Quote on request

Re-Clean Guarantee

If any area covered by the agreed scope does not pass inspection, we return to re-clean it at no additional charge.

Claims must be made within 72 hours of the clean, with access granted for the re-clean visit.

No Surprises

  • Scope confirmed in writing before booking
  • Fixed price for the agreed work
  • No hidden fees or post-clean charges
  • Checklist available on request
