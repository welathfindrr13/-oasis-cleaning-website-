Frequently asked questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services.

Are products and equipment included?

Yes, we bring all cleaning products and equipment needed for the job. We use professional-grade, eco-friendly products that are safe for your family and pets. If you have specific product preferences or allergies, just let us know when booking.

What if I'm not satisfied with the clean?

We offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If something isn't right, contact us within 24 hours and we'll return to address any concerns at no extra cost. Your happiness is our priority.

Do you serve my area in London?

We cover all London boroughs and surrounding areas. Enter your postcode in our quote form to check availability and get an accurate price. Some outer areas may have a small travel surcharge.

How do I pay for the service?

We accept bank transfer, card payment (online or over phone), or cash on completion. For regular contracts, we can set up automatic billing. Payment is due upon completion for one-off cleans, or monthly for ongoing contracts.
