Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate will my quote be?

We provide accurate estimates based on the information you provide. For complex jobs or larger properties, we may arrange a brief site visit (free of charge) to ensure absolute accuracy before you commit.

Will the final cost match the quote?

Yes—unless you request additional services or the property condition differs significantly from your description. We'll always discuss any changes before proceeding.

Can I modify my requirements after receiving a quote?

Absolutely. We'll adjust the quote to reflect any changes you'd like to make to the service scope, frequency, or timing.

Do you offer discounts for regular bookings?

Yes—weekly, fortnightly, and monthly contracts typically receive preferential rates compared to one-off cleans. Your quote will reflect this.
