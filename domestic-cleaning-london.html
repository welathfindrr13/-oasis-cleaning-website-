Domestic Cleaning

Structured home cleaning for London households

For busy professionals, families, and tenants who want consistent upkeep without the unpredictability of informal arrangements.

Who This Service Is For

  • Busy professionals who need reliable cleaning on a set schedule
  • Families who want a consistently clean home environment
  • Tenants wanting consistent upkeep to maintain property standards
  • Households without a regular cleaner looking for structured support

What's Included

Kitchen

  • Worktops and surfaces cleaned
  • Sink and taps cleaned
  • Hob and external appliances wiped
  • Cupboard fronts wiped

Bathrooms

  • Toilet cleaned and sanitised
  • Sink, taps and surfaces cleaned
  • Shower and bath cleaned
  • Mirrors wiped

Living Areas & Bedrooms

  • Surfaces dusted and wiped
  • Floors vacuumed and mopped
  • Skirting boards wiped (reachable areas)

General

  • Bins emptied
  • Light tidying where reasonable

What's Not Included

  • Internal or external window cleaning
  • Deep oven or appliance dismantling
  • Heavy mould removal
  • Construction or renovation debris

Pricing

From £28.00 per hour

Domestic cleaning is charged hourly with a minimum visit time.

Short visits are not offered. All scope, timing and pricing are confirmed before booking.

Quality & Standards

Each domestic clean follows a defined checklist and is internally reviewed for consistency.

If something within the agreed scope is missed, we return to correct it.
