What's Included

Every residential clean follows a structured professional checklist, including:

  • Full kitchen and bathroom sanitation
  • Vacuuming and mopping of all hard and soft floors
  • Cleaning of worktops, sinks, taps and splashbacks
  • Toilet, shower and bath disinfection
  • Mirror, glass and internal surface polishing
  • High-touch point cleaning (handles, switches, remotes)
  • Skirting boards, edges and corners
  • Waste removal and bin emptying

We can provide all professional cleaning products and equipment, or use your preferred brands and fragrances if you are sensitive to smells, allergies or specific products.
