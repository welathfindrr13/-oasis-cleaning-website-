Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you respond to enquiries?

We aim to respond to all phone calls immediately during business hours, and emails within 24 hours. For urgent requests, calling is always fastest.

Do you provide quotes over the phone?

Yes, for standard residential and office cleans we can often provide estimates over the phone. For more complex services or larger properties, we may arrange a quick site visit to ensure accuracy.

Can I book a same-day or emergency clean?

Subject to availability, we can sometimes accommodate same-day bookings. Call us on 020 3750 8878 to check availability for urgent requests.

What if I need to reschedule or cancel?

We understand that plans change. Please give us at least 24 hours' notice for cancellations or rescheduling to avoid any charges. Contact us by phone or email.
