About

Oasis International Cleaning Services

Founded in 2025 by Marcus Edwards, Oasis International Cleaning Services Ltd is built on a foundation of disciplined process and zero-tolerance quality control.

Our Approach

We operate on the principle that cleaning should be structured, accountable, and predictable.

Every service we provide follows a defined scope with clear expectations. We don't overpromise. We confirm exactly what's included, what's not, and what it costs—before work begins.

This approach means no surprises for you, and no ambiguity for our team.
