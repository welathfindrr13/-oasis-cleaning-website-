What's Included

Every office clean follows a commercial cleaning checklist including:

  • Desk, workstation and surface cleaning
  • Kitchen and staff-area sanitation
  • Toilet and washroom cleaning
  • Floor vacuuming and mopping
  • High-touch point disinfection
  • Bin emptying and waste handling
  • Glass and internal window cleaning

We can provide all cleaning products or use your preferred brands.

Pricing

Office cleaning is priced as either:

  • An hourly rate, or
  • A fixed monthly contract

Pricing is calculated based on:

  • Size of office
  • Frequency
  • Number of staff
  • Level of use

All pricing is generated through our quote engine or consultation.
