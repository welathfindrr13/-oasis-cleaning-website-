Who This Service Is For

  • Small to medium businesses needing regular office cleaning
  • Property managers responsible for commercial spaces
  • Office administrators looking for reliable, scheduled cleaning
  • Co-working spaces and serviced offices requiring consistent standards

What's Included

General Office Areas

  • Desks, workstations and surfaces wiped
  • Reception and common areas cleaned
  • Floors vacuumed and mopped
  • Bins emptied and liners replaced
  • Door handles and light switches sanitised

Kitchen & Break Areas

  • Worktops and surfaces cleaned
  • Sink and taps cleaned
  • Appliance exteriors wiped
  • Tables and chairs wiped down
  • Floor cleaned

Washrooms

  • Toilets, urinals and sinks sanitised
  • Mirrors cleaned
  • Dispensers refilled (supplies provided by client)
  • Floors mopped
  • Bins emptied

Meeting Rooms

  • Tables and chairs wiped
  • Surfaces dusted
  • Floors vacuumed
  • Bins emptied

What's Not Included

  • External or high-level window cleaning
  • IT equipment internal cleaning
  • Specialist floor treatments (polishing, stripping)
  • Washroom consumables (paper, soap) unless agreed
