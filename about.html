Premium cleaning for London homes and businesses — built on professionalism, reliability, and attention to detail.

Our Story

Oasis International Cleaning Services was created to raise the standard of cleaning across London. We saw that customers wanted more than a basic clean — they wanted professionalism, consistency, and a company that actually cares about results.

So we built Oasis around one mission:

"Deliver the highest-quality cleaning experience in London — every single time."

What began as a small team with a commitment to excellence has grown into a full, premium cleaning service trusted by households, tenants, landlords, and commercial clients across Greater London.
