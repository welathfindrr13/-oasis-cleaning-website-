About Oasis International Cleaning Services

Delivering premium cleaning services across London with trained staff, eco-friendly products, and a commitment to excellence since 2015.

Our Story

Oasis International Cleaning Services was founded in 2015 with a simple mission: to provide London's homes and businesses with reliable, high-quality cleaning services that deliver consistent results. We recognised a gap in the market for cleaning companies that combined professional standards with genuine care for clients' spaces and schedules.

From our first residential contract in Kensington to our current portfolio spanning offices, clinics, and properties across Greater London, we've grown by maintaining one core principle—proper planning prevents poor performance. This ethos guides every aspect of our work, from staff training to equipment selection to client communication.

Today, we're proud to serve hundreds of satisfied clients across London, from busy families seeking reliable weekly home cleans to property managers requiring comprehensive end-of-tenancy services. Our team of trained, vetted cleaners brings professionalism and attention to detail to every job, whether it's a routine maintenance visit or a specialist deep clean. We've built our reputation on consistency, integrity, and a genuine commitment to making our clients' lives easier.

Our Values

Professionalism

Every team member is thoroughly vetted, fully trained, and committed to maintaining the highest standards. We arrive on time, work efficiently, and respect your property as if it were our own. Our staff wear uniform, carry ID, and follow strict protocols for access and security.

Consistency

We don't believe in variable standards. Whether it's your first clean or your fiftieth, you'll receive the same thorough service every time. Our quality checklists and systematic approach ensure nothing is overlooked, and supervisors conduct regular spot-checks to maintain our high bar.

Sustainability

We prioritise eco-friendly cleaning products that are effective yet safe for your family, pets, and the environment. Our microfibre systems reduce chemical use, and we continuously evaluate new technologies and methods that minimise environmental impact without compromising cleaning quality.

Respect

Your space, your schedule, your preferences—we work around what matters to you. We listen carefully to specific requirements, communicate proactively if schedules need adjusting, and always seek permission before making changes. Your trust is something we earn with every visit.
